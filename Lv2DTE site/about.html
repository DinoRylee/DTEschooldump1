About

Aperture creatives is about taking old overlooked pieces of furniture and or decor and giving them a new look.
Sometimes they take a complete fix up and makeover and sometimes something as simple as changing the handles can change the entire look.
Some people think it’s wrong to paint or change a piece of furniture that’s been in your family for some time, but it’s still the same piece of furniture, sometimes you just want it to “fit” better with your other pieces.
